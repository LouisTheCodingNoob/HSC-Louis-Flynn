Additionally, the Rebate Team was recognized for its exceptional collaboration and performance, earning the highest developer satisfaction rating among all teams at Do it Best. Southwest Allen County Fire District
The Southwest Allen County Fire District faced challenges with three outdated workstations that were critical for accounting and fire station management tasks. My company was hired to research and recommend new business-grade laptops that would meet their operational needs while staying within budget. After finalizing the recommendations, I traveled on-site to set up and configure the new workstations, ensuring a seamless transition with minimal disruption to their daily operations. The result was a significant boost in productivity for the fire station, as the new systems allowed staff to work more efficiently and reliably. Additionally, the project was completed 50% under the original budget.
